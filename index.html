Chapter IV

Daily pillars.

Not motivation. Systems. Not intensity. Repetition.

01
Study & Career Progress
45 minutes of real work. On terrible days — 15 minutes. Never zero.
45 min
02
Reading
10 pages. Fiction, philosophy, biography, psychology. Your brain needs depth, not only dopamine.
10 pages
03
Movement
20 minutes or 5,000 steps. The goal isn't aesthetics. The goal is energy, confidence, emotional stability.
20 min
04
Nourish Well
More water. One fruit. Protein in at least one meal. No extreme rules — just gentle consistency.
daily
05
Language Learning
10–15 minutes. Consistency matters more than duration. Keep the streak, not the session length.
15 min
06
Skincare & Grooming
Morning and night. Facewash, serum, sunscreen. Don't overcomplicate. Two minutes, twice daily.
2 min
07
Intentional Entertainment
One episode, one film, music you love. Not banned — just conscious. Consume intentionally, not endlessly.
1 thing
08
Journal & Tomorrow's Plan
5 minutes at night. What went right. What felt hard. One improvement. Three tasks for tomorrow.
5 min
09
Rest, Protect Sleep
Sleep before the scroll destroys your brain. The morning version of you will be grateful every time.
non-neg.

Hit 7 out of 9 every day.
Miss a day? Fine.
Never miss twice.
